In the middle of it, stood the shelter cabin.

While kicking up the snow, I ran down the gradual slope.

I finally arrived in front of the shelter.

I tried to peek into the storehouse.

It means those 3 died on the 11th of January.

The room was painted pure white without a single stain.

壁には、鉄格子の嵌められた小さな窓があった。%K%P
On the wall, there was a small barred window.

Outside, I could see the cherry blossoms falling and dancing nervously on the wind.
